Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as a gym member? +
You can register online by visiting the Sign Up page on our website or by visiting the gym's front desk.
What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and digital wallets like PayPal and Google Pay.
How can I book a session with a trainer? +
You can book a session through the Trainer Booking section in your account.
Are gym equipment and machines regularly maintained? +
Yes, all equipment is cleaned and maintained daily to ensure safety and hygiene.
What facilities are available in the gym? +
Our gym includes cardio machines, weight training areas, personal training, group classes, a swimming pool, and a sauna.
How do I reset my password? +
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it.
Is there a locker facility available? +
Yes, lockers are available for members. Please bring your own lock or rent one at the reception.
